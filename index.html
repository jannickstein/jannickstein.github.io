Hi, I’m Jannick.

I invest in exceptional people to build startups from scratch at Entrepreneurs First. I also run Chiefs Of Stuff, a community of hundreds of generalist operators.

I believe generalists run the world. They embody ambition, curiosity, and, most importantly, versatility. To learn more about generalism, read my essay The Generalist Edge.

Previously, I spent four years working as a Chief of Staff and startup generalist at Ledgy and Acapela. I also learned a lot working in venture capital at Global Founders Capital, Earlybird, and b2venture. I graduated from the University of St. Gallen with a BA in Business Administration.

I grew up in Northern Germany and currently live in London. I love coffee, chess, and drumming.

I like to meet new people, especially if they’re opinionated. Reach out via Twitter, LinkedIn, or email.
